# GTM

## Who this product is for

The MVP is aimed at small teams that are early in AI tooling adoption and starting to feel tool sprawl.

Best fit:

- startup founders
- operators
- engineering managers
- small product teams
- teams paying for multiple seat-based AI subscriptions

## Core pain point

The product is built around one simple problem:

“Are we paying for more AI software than we actually use?”

That is narrow enough to explain clearly, but real enough to feel useful.

## Early distribution ideas

### 1. Founder and operator communities

- indie hacker communities
- startup Slack groups
- founder WhatsApp or Discord groups
- product and ops communities on LinkedIn or X

### 2. Content-led distribution

- short teardown posts about AI tool waste
- simple calculators shared as screenshots
- “we reviewed 10 startup AI stacks” style posts

### 3. Warm outbound for service conversion

Since the product ends with a Credex consultation CTA, a realistic motion is:

- get teams to run the free audit
- capture lead info on the result page
- follow up with a manual review or advisory call

## Positioning

Not:

- a full procurement suite
- a finance ERP feature
- an enterprise IT governance product

Instead:

- a lightweight AI spend check for small teams
- a lead-gen wedge into a deeper service conversation

## Why this GTM makes sense for the assignment

It fits the scope of the MVP:

- one focused workflow
- one clear value proposition
- one follow-up CTA

That is more believable than pretending this is already a full SaaS platform.
